ABOUT US

Welcome to MyPet, where every pet deserves a second chance! Our mission is to connect loving families with pets in need, creating lifelong bonds built on care and companionship.

Since our founding in 2015, we’ve been dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals, one wagging tail and purring heart at a time.

With the help of our passionate team and community, we’ve already found homes for over 2,000 pets—and we’re just getting started! Visit us to meet your future best friend, learn about adoption, or get involved through volunteering.

Together, we can give every pet the love they deserve.
